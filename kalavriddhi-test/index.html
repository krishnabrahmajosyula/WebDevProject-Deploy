Welcome to Kalavriddhi!

We’re excited to have you here! KalaVriddhi is a space where art and culture come to life. Here’s how you can get started:

  • Explore the Map of India: Play around with the map of our special interactive cultural map of India to get to know about the various cultural art and dance forms of various states.
  • Learn Bharatanatyam: Dive deep into the basics of Bharatanatyam through engaging tutorials, quizzes and a very special dance simulator! All of this enhanced with the power of 3-d models!
  • Learn with Games: Make learning fun with our interactive games designed to teach Indian culture and art. Give your best shot and secure a place in ur leaderboard!
  • Heritage Hunt Quiz: Test your knowledge about India's rich cultural heritage with our small quiz of five random questions! QUestions shuffle each time!

If you have ideas or questions, feel free to use the "Request a Feature" and "Contribute Questions" options. They help us help you out!
